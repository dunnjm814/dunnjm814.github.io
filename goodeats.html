Good Eats

A Goodreads clone designed for culinary excellence.

What is GoodEats?

GoodEats is the cumulative effort by Taten Knight, CB Barnes, Jesse Lindloff, and Jason Dunn for the App Academy full stack Online curriculum. A clone of GoodReads dedicated to culinary arts. A user can log in, search recipes within the application. If a user wants to cook a recipe, they may add it to a cookbook for reference, and they may also state on the recipe whether or not they actually cooked it. Reviews and ratings may be left on the recipes, which are all rendered with AJAX.
