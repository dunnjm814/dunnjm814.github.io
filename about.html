About me

Software engineering, science, and hospitality industry professional,
audio-visual creative.

Hey there! I'm glad you're here.

My name is Jason, my passion for all things creative are infinite. I fell in love with the idea of the Internet at a young age, updating my Myspace page to house my custom banners and play my favorite songs. This integration of audio and visual media in an online space took over and defined my nature for years to come.

I love the outdoors!

I've lived in the great lakes state my whole life, and in 2019 I made the decision to move to Arizona. Exploring the landscape and learning the vegetation here is a great way to practice mindfulness. A lot of wealth of inspiration can come from a fresh perspective.

I like to play music!

Music is meditation to me. It primes my brain for the daily grind. Just like with software development, there are many tools to achieve the reward of a beautiful tune. I have been playing and performing for a combined 16 years! My primary software is Ableton Live, and I love integrating that environment with Native Instruments Machine.
